RING DYNASTY - Gacha Rate And Wrestler Grade Reference

1. Current normal gacha rates

Default rates with no legacy upgrades:
- C: 47.4%
- B: 30.0%
- A: 20.0%
- S: 2.0%
- LEGEND: 0.6%

Relevant code:
- A rate comes from `0.20 + (gachaRateLevel * 0.001)`
- S rate comes from `0.02 + (gachaRateLevel * 0.0015)`
- LEGEND rate comes from `0.006 + (legendChanceLevel * 0.005) + (gachaRateLevel * 0.002)`

In percent form:
- A = 20.0 + (gachaRateLevel * 0.1)
- S = 2.0 + (gachaRateLevel * 0.15)
- LEGEND = 0.6 + (gachaRateLevel * 0.2) + (legendChanceLevel * 0.5)
- B = 30.0 + (gachaRateLevel * 0.05)
- C = 100.0 - (LEGEND + S + A + B)

Upgrade caps in current code:
- gachaRate max level: 5
- legendChance max level: 3

Max possible normal rates with current upgrade caps:
- C: 43.65%
- B: 30.25%
- A: 20.5%
- S: 2.75%
- LEGEND: 2.85%

2. Pity / guarantee rules

Base pity targets:
- B+ pity: every 10 pulls
- A/S/LEGEND pity: every 80 pulls
- LEGEND pity: every 200 pulls

`pityReduce` lowers pity by 5 per level:
- B+ pity: `max(5, 10 - pityReduceLevel * 5)`
- A/S/LEGEND pity: `max(40, 80 - pityReduceLevel * 5)`
- LEGEND pity: `max(80, 200 - pityReduceLevel * 10)`

With current max `pityReduce` level 5:
- B+ pity minimum: 5
- A/S/LEGEND pity minimum: 55
- LEGEND pity minimum: 150

Special pity results:
- B+ pity roll:
  - LEGEND: 5%
  - S: 10%
  - A: 25%
  - B: 60%
- A/S/LEGEND pity roll:
  - A: 60%
  - S: 30%
  - LEGEND: 10%
- LEGEND pity roll:
  - LEGEND: 100%

3. Notes about the wrestler pool

- This list is generated from the current `PORTRAIT_FILES` pool and grade rules.
- Excluded non-playable portrait ids are not listed.
- `Apex Legacy` is not part of the base pool. It is a prestige unlock and enters the pool only at prestige count 3 or higher.
- `Bad Bunny` and `Bianca Belair` were already A-grade. Their popularity was lowered inside the A range so their generated stats also go down.
